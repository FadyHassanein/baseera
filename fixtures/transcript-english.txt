I've been a manual wheelchair user for about eight years. I can transfer to a chair or toilet on my own as long as there's at least three feet of clear space next to whatever I'm transferring to. Doorways under thirty-two inches are tight. I also have some vision loss in my right eye that mostly affects me in dim lighting. Stairs are obviously a no-go, and I prefer venues where I can park close to the entrance because pushing long distances over uneven ground tires me out.
